Cards Against 2019 Resources

Here are templates to make your own Cards Against 2019 deck! Just replace the existing text with your text! If the "Cards Against 2019" logo doesn't line up right, try hitting "enter" and that should make it line up. If you don't want to add anything, then just print out the existing templates without making any changes.

To print the deck, I would reccomend exporting the card templates as a PDF and going to a local print shop (like a FedEx Kinkos), and then ask them to print out the PDF on a thick cardstock. I used 80 lb Laser Cardstock, but if I would do it over again, I would have done it on an even thicker cardstock to make the cards more durable, but that does just mean it will be more expensive. If you plan on playing only a couple rounds, then just printing out on some plain printer paper will be fine as long as you're ok with them looking a little lower quality. You should only need to have the print job done in black and white, but if you can convince the guys at the print shop, they will be nice and let you print the black and white on their color printer, which will output a higher quality piece. Once you've printed out the template, you then need to cut it. If you want to save yourself 1-1.5 hours of work, go ahead and pay the nice men at the print shop to use their guillotine-style cutter to cut the deck for you, but it will cost rougly ~$16-18 in cutting costs alone!
